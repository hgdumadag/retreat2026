Day 1 Notes & Reflections

  • God surprise me!
  • First Talk - Fr. Albert: brewing coffee, Emmanuel, and the Woman at the Well
  • Randy's Talk: the world needs more of our presence than our programs

Key takeaways from Fr. Albert

  • How God finds us is the heart of Christianity; our call is to allow ourselves to be found.
  • The Woman at the Well mirrors our tendency to build walls, while Jesus meets us gently and breaks them one by one.
  • The love that truly satisfies has already found us in God's presence.
  • Humility is required: to be seen and loved without needing to earn it.

Where I notice my thirst

  • Recognition through serving, performing, and visible achievements.
  • Growth goals: AI, scripture, catechism, certifications, projects, and wellness disciplines.
  • Longing for financial freedom.

Reflection and prayer

In the places where I struggle and where I do not want to be, God remains with me patiently. He does not rush me. He stays as long as needed until I recognize His lesson and love.

When I feel alone, ashamed, or broken, God is beside me. The way I held Geordan in his fear and pain reminds me that God holds me with even greater mercy and tenderness.

Randy's Talk: symbols and sending

  • The world needs our presence more than our programs.
  • Towel as a reminder of a gift already given: we are already saved by grace.
  • Basin, water, towel: stoop and wash in loving service.
  • Washing of hands and feet (with wet wipes) as a lived gesture of humility.
